Privacy Policy

At BOB Deck Manufacturing, we are committed to safeguarding the privacy of our website visitors. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your personal information when you interact with our website.

1. Information We Collect

We collect various types of information to better serve you and improve our website. The information we collect includes:

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, phone number, and other contact details provided when you interact with us.
  • Usage Data: Non-personal information like IP address, browser type, device information, and the pages you visit on our site.
  • Cookies: We use cookies to track preferences, improve your user experience, and provide personalized services.

2. How We Use Your Information

The information we collect helps us:

  • Personalize your experience and respond to your inquiries.
  • Enhance our website's functionality and content.
  • Send you marketing materials if you opt-in.
  • Ensure website security and prevent fraudulent activities.

3. Cookies

Cookies are small files stored on your device that help us improve your browsing experience. You can control how cookies are used by adjusting your browser settings.

4. Data Security

We are committed to ensuring the security of your data. We have implemented appropriate physical, electronic, and managerial procedures to prevent unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure of your personal information.

5. Sharing Information

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. However, we may share your data with trusted third-party service providers who assist us in operating our website or conducting our business, provided they agree to keep your information confidential.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to access, update, or request the deletion of your personal information at any time. You may also request to limit the processing of your data. To exercise these rights, please contact us.

7. Updates to this Policy

We reserve the right to update this privacy policy periodically. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this privacy policy, please contact us at info@bobdeckmanufacturing.com.
